The Practitioner

A career built at the intersection of industry, academia, and human potential.

From the foundry floor to the faculty room — and back again.

Mr. Kandepu's professional journey traverses Construction, Engineering, Infrastructure Development, Power & Energy, Education, Chemicals, Metro Rail, and Media — in India and abroad. The thread that binds them is a steady conviction: that strategy and people are one conversation, not two.

Across his career he has worked with Siemens India, the L&T Group, Vishnu Chemicals, Shapoorji Pallonji Group in Muscat (Sultanate of Oman), and most recently spearheaded Strategy, OD and Leadership Coaching at Power Mech Projects Limited, Hyderabad. In academia, he played a founding role at KL University, particularly in establishing the Strategy Framework for KLH Global Business School, Hyderabad.

Talent acquisition is not a function but an art — it creates ample opportunity while supporting business continuity.

A Motivational Speaker with over 650+ sessions delivered in academia — covering Student Affairs, Career Counselling and Youth Development — Mr. Kandepu has authored numerous Knowledge Articles and conducted Industry Surveys, Compensation Benchmarking Exercises, and Best Practice Studies in Human Resources and General Management.

He is a Certified Job Evaluation Professional from Mercer HR Consulting and a Certified Case Writer in Teaching from ISB. His expertise in writing case studies — both factual and hypothetical — has been recognised by XLRI Jamshedpur and IIM Ahmedabad.

He is currently engaged independently as a Strategy Adviser & Leadership Coach, pursuing initiatives across Leadership Development, Organisational Development, L&D, Headhunting, and Productivity Optimisation, with assignments in India, Dubai and the broader UAE/GCC region.
